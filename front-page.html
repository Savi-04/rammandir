Ram Mandir, often referred to as the Ayodhya Ram Temple, is located in Ayodhya, a historic city in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India. Ayodhya, according to the ancient Hindu epic Ramayana, is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, a major deity in the Hindu pantheon and the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu.
